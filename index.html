Information About BPDC

BITS Dubai is a branch of BITS Pilani, India, offering UG and PG programs in engineering, management, and technology. Known for its strong academic framework, industry collaborations, and vibrant student life, BITS Dubai provides opportunities for internships and projects in the UAE's rapidly growing sectors. The campus is located in DIAC, offering state-of-the-art facilities for research, labs, and extracurricular activities.
